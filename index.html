About me

I'm currently a fifth year PhD student of Parallel Data Lab at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Greg Ganger and Phil Gibbons.

My research is focused on sytems for Machine Learning. In paritcualr I am interested in desigining ML systems
that are able to efficiently execute in shared computing enviroments, such as AWS EC2 and Microsoft Azure.

Before coming to CMU I completed my undergraduate study at Northeastern University.
